Agricultural Services

Expert guidance and practical solutions for crop planting and poultry rearing.

At AFOD VENTURES, we provide comprehensive agricultural services tailored to help you succeed in crop farming and poultry production. Our expert guides cover everything from land preparation to harvesting, and from chick rearing to egg collection.

Crops

Our crop planting services provide you with step-by-step guidance on establishing and managing successful crop farms. We cover the entire planting cycle to ensure you achieve optimal yields.

  • Land Preparation: Soil testing, clearing, and proper tillage techniques
  • Seed Selection & Planting: Choosing the right varieties and optimal planting methods
  • Fertilizer Application: Correct timing and types for maximum nutrient uptake
  • Irrigation Management: Water scheduling and conservation techniques
  • Weed & Pest Control: Integrated management for healthy crops
  • Harvesting & Post-Harvest: Best practices to reduce losses and maintain quality
Explore Crop Guides

Poultry

Our poultry rearing services offer comprehensive guidance for raising healthy, productive flocks. Whether you're starting with broilers, layers, or local chickens, we provide practical, step-by-step support.

  • Housing & Equipment: Proper shelter design and essential equipment setup
  • Chick Sourcing & Rearing: Selecting quality chicks and managing brooding stages
  • Feeding & Nutrition: Balanced feed formulations for optimal growth and production
  • Vaccination & Health: Preventive care and disease management protocols
  • Egg Production & Collection: Maximizing layer productivity and quality
  • Marketing & Sales: Connecting you with profitable market opportunities
